## PHASE 3: SALES PROCESS (Ongoing)

### Discovery Call Framework (30 mins)

**1. Context (5 mins)**
*   "Tell me about your business — what do you do, who do you serve?"
*   "What's working well right now?"

**2. Pain Points (10 mins)**
*   "Where are the bottlenecks? What's eating up your time?"
*   "How are you currently handling [leads/follow-up/onboarding]?"
*   "What happens when you're busy or away?"

**3. Impact (5 mins)**
*   "If we solved [pain point], what would that mean for you?"
*   "What is it costing you to not have this fixed?"

**4. Solution Preview (5 mins)**
*   "Based on what you've shared, here's what I'd recommend..."
*   Show relevant case study or example
*   "Does this feel like it would solve your problem?"

**5. Next Steps (5 mins)**
*   "Next step is a deeper audit of your current setup. I'll map out exactly what's broken and what we'd build. Takes 45 minutes."
*   Book audit call or send proposal depending on readiness

### Proposal Structure

**Section 1: What We Heard**
*   Summary of their pain points in their words

**Section 2: Recommended Solution**
*   Specific automations and systems
*   Timeline (typically 2–4 weeks for setup)

**Section 3: Investment**
*   Clear pricing
*   Payment terms (50% upfront for new clients)

**Section 4: Next Steps**
*   "Reply with 'Let's go' and I'll send the agreement and invoice"

## PHASE 4: BOOKING & SALES PROCESS

**Response Time:** Under 10 minutes during waking hours

**First Response Template:**
> Hi! Thanks for reaching out about Hayahaya Adventures 🚙
>
> Quick questions to help me recommend the best package:
> 1. How many people?
> 2. When are you planning to go?
> 3. Do you need just the Jimny, or are you interested in glamping/Starlink too?
>
> Looking forward to getting you set up for an epic trip!

**Qualification:**
*   Budget check: Are they price shopping or experience shopping?
*   Timeline: Urgent bookings (next 2 weeks) vs. planners
*   Group size: Match to right bundle

**Closing Script:**
> Perfect! The Weekend Escape bundle fits what you're looking for.
>
> Here's what you get:
> ✓ Jimny 5-door (automatic, easy to drive)
> ✓ Full glamping setup (tent, bedding, lights)
> ✓ Pickup [date] at [location]
> ✓ Return [date]
>
> Total: 8,500 PHP
> To lock it in, I just need a 50% deposit (4,250 PHP) via GCash/Maya.
>
> Ready to book?

### Post-Booking Experience

**Immediately After Booking:**
*   Send confirmation with:
    *   Pickup location and time
    *   What to bring checklist
    *   Rental agreement to sign
    *   Emergency contact

**2 Days Before:**
*   Reminder message
*   Weather check
*   Final headcount confirmation

**During Rental:**
*   Check-in message: "How's the adventure going?"
*   Be available for questions

**Return Day:**
*   Inspect Jimny with customer present
*   Get feedback: "What was the highlight? What could be better?"
