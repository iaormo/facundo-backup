## SALES PROCESS

### Discovery Call (30 mins)

1. **Context (5m):** Business overview
2. **Pain Points (10m):** Bottlenecks, current handling
3. **Impact (5m):** Cost of not solving
4. **Solution Preview (5m):** Show case study
5. **Next Steps (5m):** Present packages, close or schedule audit

### One-Call Close Approach

**No proposals.** Verbal agreement, then invoice.

**Pre-qualify hard:** Only talk to people who can pay and have the problem.

**Cash upfront:** No net 30, no "let me think about it."

**The Close:**
> "I'll build you a system that handles your lead follow-up automatically. It costs $2,500 to set up and $1,000/month. If it doesn't save you 10 hours a month in 60 days, month 3 is free. Does that work or does that not work?"

Simple. Direct. No friction.

## OBJECTION HANDLING

### "It's too expensive"

> "I get it. Let me ask — how many leads did you lose last month because of slow follow-up? If this system saves just one sale a month, it pays for itself. Most clients see ROI within 30 days."

### "I need to think about it"

> "Totally understand. What specifically are you weighing? Is it the investment, the timing, or something else?"

[Address the real concern, then:]

> "Here's what I can do — let's start with the audit. No commitment. If it makes sense after that, we move forward. If not, you still walk away with 3 fixes you can implement yourself."

### "I've tried automation before and it didn't work"

> "Let me guess — it was either too complicated to set up, or nobody used it after launch? That's exactly why we do things differently. We build for adoption, not just functionality. Want to see how?"
