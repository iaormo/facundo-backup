# ScalePlus.io Social Media Strategy
## Facebook & Instagram Growth Playbook

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Created:** February 2026  
**Platforms:** Facebook + Instagram  
**Target:** SMEs (Small & Medium Enterprises)  
**Positioning:** Premium Freelancer | Outcome-Based | Low Friction

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

ScalePlus.io helps SMEs automate their business operations, capture more leads, and scale without hiring. This strategy positions the brand as the go-to authority for business automation, delivering valuable, no-fluff content that drives qualified leads to book sales calls.

**Primary Goal:** Generate 15-25 qualified sales calls per month from social media within 90 days.

---

## 2. PLATFORM-SPECIFIC STRATEGY

### 2.1 FACEBOOK STRATEGY

**Why Facebook?**
- Primary decision-makers (35-55) are active here
- Groups and community building opportunities
- Longer-form content performs well
- Advertising retargeting capabilities

**Content Approach:**
- **Educational authority posts** (3x/week): Case studies, how-to guides, industry insights
- **Community engagement** (daily): Active in SME/business owner groups
- **Live content** (1x/week): Q&A sessions, "automation audits," behind-the-scenes
- **Client success stories** (2x/week): Video testimonials, before/after breakdowns

**Facebook-Specific Tactics:**
1. **Facebook Groups Strategy:**
   - Join 10-15 SME-focused groups (business owners, entrepreneurs, industry-specific)
   - Provide value 90% of the time, soft pitch 10%
   - Answer automation-related questions to establish authority
   - Post native video content directly to groups (higher reach)

2. **Facebook Page Optimization:**
   - Cover photo: Clear value prop + CTA to book call
   - Featured video: 60-second "What is ScalePlus?" explainer
   - Services tab: Directly linked to pricing/packages
   - Reviews: Aggressively collect and showcase video testimonials

3. **Facebook Live Schedule:**
   - "Automation Audit Fridays" - Review a follower's business live
   - "Ask Me Anything" sessions on workflow automation
   - Behind-the-scenes of builds and implementations

### 2.2 INSTAGRAM STRATEGY

**Why Instagram?**
- Visual platform perfect for showcasing results
- Reels algorithm favors valuable business content
- Stories for daily touchpoints and urgency
- DM automation for lead qualification

**Content Approach:**
- **Reels** (5-6x/week): Quick tips, myth-busting, client wins, "how they did it"
- **Carousels** (3x/week): Step-by-step guides, frameworks, case study breakdowns
- **Stories** (daily): Polls, Q&A, behind-the-scenes, countdowns, testimonials
- **Feed posts** (2-3x/week): Longer captions with strong CTAs

## 3. CONTENT PILLARS

### PILLAR 1: The Reality Check (Myth-Busting)
**Purpose:** Challenge misconceptions, grab attention, position as truth-teller

**Topics:**
- "Automation isn't just for big companies"
- "Why your CRM is actually losing you leads"
- "The 'set it and forget it' automation lie"
- "Why hiring more staff won't fix your bottleneck"

**Formats:** Reels, single-image posts, Stories polls

**Example Hook:** "Your $50K CRM is worthless if you're doing this one thing wrong..."

**Purpose:** Social proof, demonstrate ROI, inspire action

**Formats:** Carousels, Reels, video testimonials, case study posts

### HASHTAG BUCKETS

**Bucket A: Broad Business (3-5 hashtags)**
- #SmallBusiness #BusinessOwner #Entrepreneur #SME #BusinessGrowth

**Bucket B: Niche Specific (3-5 hashtags)**
- #BusinessAutomation #WorkflowAutomation #CRM #LeadGeneration #Systems

**Bucket C: Outcome Focused (2-3 hashtags)**
- #ScaleYourBusiness #BusinessEfficiency #AutomationTools

**Bucket D: Community/Engagement (2-3 hashtags)**
- #[YourCity]Business #BusinessTips #SMEAdvice

**Bucket E: Branded (1-2 hashtags)**
- #ScalePlus #ScalePlusAutomation

### HASHTAG RULES

1. Use 10-15 hashtags max (Instagram allows 30, but 10-15 performs better now)
2. Mix sizes: 30% small (1K-10K), 50% medium (10K-100K), 20% large (100K+)
3. Place in caption (not first comment - myth debunked)
4. Rotate sets - do not use the exact same 15 every time
5. Create branded hashtag - #ScalePlusSuccess for client wins

## 7. ENGAGEMENT TACTICS

### DAILY ENGAGEMENT ROUTINE (30-45 minutes)

**Morning (15 min):**
- Respond to all DMs and comments from last 12 hours
- Reply to 10-15 Stories from ideal clients/partners
- Comment meaningfully on 5 posts from target accounts

**Midday (10 min):**
- Check notifications, respond to new comments
- Engage with 5 posts in Facebook groups
- Post to Stories

**Evening (15-20 min):**
- Respond to all remaining comments
- Engage with 10 posts from ideal clients
- Proactive outreach to 3-5 new potential clients
- Reply to Story interactions

### DM STRATEGY

**Inbound DMs (Response Framework):**

1. **Acknowledge + Appreciate (within 2 hours)**
   - "Thanks for reaching out!"
   - "Appreciate you messaging me"

2. **Address their specific question/comment**
   - Reference what they said
   - Show you actually read their message

3. **Provide value or answer**
   - Be helpful first
   - Do not pitch immediately

4. **Soft qualification**
   - "What type of business are you running?"
   - "What automation are you currently using?"

5. **CTA to next step**
   - Send to booking link
   - Offer resource
   - Suggest call

### FACEBOOK GROUP STRATEGY

**Groups to Join:**
- Local business owner groups
- Industry-specific SME groups
- Entrepreneurship communities
- Marketing/automation groups
- Your target industry's professional groups

**Engagement Rules:**
1. **Lurk first** - Understand group culture and rules
2. **Add value 10x before posting** - Build reputation
3. **Answer questions** - Be the helpful expert
4. **Share wins** - Client successes, lessons learned
5. **Soft CTAs** - "DM me if you want the template"
6. **Never hard sell** - Leads to ban/block
