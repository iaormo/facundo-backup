## PHASE 2: PRODUCT PACKAGING (Week 2–3)

### Bundle Strategy

**Core Principle:** Individual rentals are options. Bundles are the selling focus.

| Bundle | Contents | Price | Target |
|--------|----------|-------|--------|
| **Weekend Escape** | Jimny + Glamping setup (2 nights) | 8,500 PHP | Couples, small families |
| **Digital Nomad Kit** | Jimny + Starlink + EcoFlow | 5,500/day | Remote workers, content creators |
| **Full Adventure** | Jimny + Glamping + Starlink | 10,000 (2 nights) | Groups, influencers |
| **Jimny Only** | Vehicle only | 3,500/day | Budget option |

### Add-Ons (Increase AOV)

*   Extra camping gear (chairs, tables, cookware): +500 PHP/day
*   Airport pickup/dropoff: +1,500 PHP
*   Pre-loaded itinerary with campsites: +1,000 PHP
*   Photographer/content creator for the trip: +3,000 PHP/day

### Pricing Psychology

*   Show individual prices crossed out, bundle price highlighted
*   "Save 2,000 PHP with the Weekend Escape bundle"
*   Require 50% deposit to book, balance 3 days before pickup

## PHASE 3: LAUNCH MARKETING (Weeks 3–6)

### Pre-Launch Buildup (Week 3–4)

**Teaser Content (Daily):**
*   Show the Jimny being prepped
*   Unboxing/setup of glamping gear
*   Starlink speed tests in scenic locations
*   Behind the scenes of preparation
*   **CTA:** "DM us to be first to know when bookings open"

**Waitlist Building:**
*   Post: "We're launching Hayahaya Adventures next week. Adventure rental with Jimny + glamping + Starlink for remote locations. Want first access? Comment 'ADVENTURE' below."
*   Collect WhatsApp numbers of interested people
*   Message them personally when bookings open

### Launch Week (Week 5)

**Day 1–2: Soft Launch**
*   Message waitlist: "Bookings are now open. First 5 bookings get 20% off."
*   Post announcement on all channels
*   Reach out to 10 friends/family for first bookings (even at discount for testimonials)

**Day 3–7: Full Push**
*   Daily content showing the experience
*   Respond to every comment/DM within 30 minutes
*   Ask early customers for reviews immediately after return

### Channel Strategy

**Primary: Facebook & Instagram**
*   Target: Adventure seekers, camping enthusiasts, Filipino travelers
*   Content types:
    *   Scenic location shots with the setup
    *   User-generated content (customers tagged)
    *   Tips: "Best campsites within 2 hours of Manila"
    *   Starlink "office anywhere" angle for remote workers

**Secondary: TikTok (High Potential)**
*   Short videos of:
    *   Jimny driving to scenic spots
    *   Time-lapse of glamping setup
    *   "POV: You brought your office to the mountains"
    *   Starlink speed test in remote location
*   Hashtags: #vanlife #glamping #adventure #philippines #starlink

**Partnerships:**
*   Reach out to 10 micro-influencers (10K–50K followers) in travel/adventure niche
*   Offer: Free Weekend Escape bundle in exchange for content + post
*   Select criteria: Engagement rate >3%, audience matches target

## PHASE 4: BOOKING & SALES PROCESS

### Inquiry Handling (WhatsApp/FB Messenger)

**Response Time:** Under 10 minutes during waking hours

**First Response Template:**
> Hi! Thanks for reaching out about Hayahaya Adventures 🚙
>
> Quick questions to help me recommend the best package:
> 1. How many people?
> 2. When are you planning to go?
> 3. Do you need just the Jimny, or are you interested in glamping/Starlink too?
>
> Looking forward to getting you set up for an epic trip!

**Qualification:**
*   Budget check: Are they price shopping or experience shopping?
*   Timeline: Urgent bookings (next 2 weeks) vs. planners
*   Group size: Match to right bundle

**Closing Script:**
> Perfect! The Weekend Escape bundle fits what you're looking for.
>
> Here's what you get:
> ✓ Jimny 5-door (automatic, easy to drive)
> ✓ Full glamping setup (tent, bedding, lights)
> ✓ Pickup [date] at [location]
> ✓ Return [date]
>
> Total: 8,500 PHP
> To lock it in, I just need a 50% deposit (4,250 PHP) via GCash/Maya.
>
> Ready to book?

### Post-Booking Experience

**Immediately After Booking:**
*   Send confirmation with:
    *   Pickup location and time
    *   What to bring checklist
    *   Rental agreement to sign
    *   Emergency contact

**2 Days Before:**
*   Reminder message
*   Weather check
*   Final headcount confirmation

**During Rental:**
*   Check-in message: "How's the adventure going?"
*   Be available for questions

**Return Day:**
*   Inspect Jimny with customer present
*   Get feedback: "What was the highlight? What could be better?"
*   Request review: "Would you mind leaving us a review on Facebook? It helps other adventurers find us."

## PHASE 5: GROWTH & EXPANSION (Month 2–3)

### Review Generation System

**Ask at Return:** In person, while they're still excited
**Follow-up (Day After):** WhatsApp message with direct link to review
**Incentive:** 500 PHP off next booking for photo/video review

### Referral Program

**"Adventure Squad" Program:**
*   Past customers get unique referral code
*   Friend books with code → Friend gets 10% off, customer gets 1,000 PHP credit
*   Track in simple spreadsheet

### Content Calendar (Ongoing)

**Weekly Schedule:**

| Day | Content Type | Example |
|-----|--------------|---------|
| Monday | Motivation/Scenic | Beautiful location shot with quote |
| Tuesday | Tips/Educational | "How to find legal campsites in the Philippines" |
| Wednesday | User Content | Customer photo/review reshare |
| Thursday | Product Focus | Close-up of glamping setup details |
| Friday | Weekend Push | "Still time to book this weekend's escape" |
| Saturday | Adventure Vibes | Real-time trip content if available |
| Sunday | Planning | "Where should we explore next?" poll |

### Seasonal Campaigns

**Valentine's Weekend (Feb 14–16):** Couples package, romantic setup add-on
**Summer (March–May):** Beach destination focus
**Rainy Season (June–Oct):** Mountain/covered campsite focus, "off-season" discounts
**Ber Months:** Holiday trip packages

| Referral bookings | 15% of bookings | Monthly |
