## PHASE 2: LEAD GENERATION (Weeks 3–6)

### Channel 1: LinkedIn Outreach (Primary)

**Target Profile:**
*   Business owners/CEOs in Philippines
*   Company size: 5–50 employees
*   Industries: Service businesses, e-commerce, professional services
*   Signals: Recently posted about growth challenges, hiring struggles, or customer service

**Daily Activity (30 mins/day):**
1.  **Engage First:** Comment meaningfully on 5 posts from targets
2.  **Connect:** Send 10 personalized connection requests
3.  **Follow-up:** 2–3 days after connect, send value-first message

**Connection Request Template:**
> Hi [Name], saw your post about [specific topic]. As someone who helps SMEs automate their operations, I find your perspective on [detail] really interesting. Would love to connect.

**First Message (After Accept):**
> Thanks for connecting, [Name]. I help SMEs build automation systems that handle lead follow-up, customer onboarding, and repetitive tasks without adding headcount. 
>
> Quick question: Are you currently drowning in manual work, or have you got systems running smoothly?
>
> Either way, no pitch — just curious where you're at.

**Goal:** 50 connections/week → 10 conversations → 2 discovery calls

### Channel 2: Facebook Groups (Secondary)

**Target Groups:**
*   Filipino Entrepreneur communities
*   SME/Startup Philippines groups
*   Industry-specific groups (e.g., real estate, e-commerce)

**Strategy:**
*   **Don't sell.** Answer questions about automation, efficiency, systems
*   **Signature CTA:** "If you want to see how this applies to your business, DM me"
*   **Frequency:** 3–5 helpful comments per day

**Sample Response:**
> This is exactly why automation matters. Most businesses lose 30% of leads just from slow follow-up.
>
> Simple fix: Auto-responder that fires immediately, then a 3-email sequence over 48 hours. Takes 2 hours to set up, works 24/7.
>
> Happy to share the exact template if anyone wants it — just DM me.

### Channel 3: Referral System (Ongoing)

**Ask Every Client:**
> "Who else do you know who's struggling with [specific pain point they had]? I'd love to help them too."

**Referral Incentive:**
*   1 month free on Growth System for every referred client who signs
*   Or: 5,000 PHP cash commission

## PHASE 3: SALES PROCESS (Ongoing)

### Discovery Call Framework (30 mins)

**1. Context (5 mins)**
*   "Tell me about your business — what do you do, who do you serve?"
*   "What's working well right now?"

**2. Pain Points (10 mins)**
*   "Where are the bottlenecks? What's eating up your time?"
*   "How are you currently handling [leads/follow-up/onboarding]?"
*   "What happens when you're busy or away?"

**3. Impact (5 mins)**
*   "If we solved [pain point], what would that mean for you?"
*   "What is it costing you to not have this fixed?"

**4. Solution Preview (5 mins)**
*   "Based on what you've shared, here's what I'd recommend..."
*   Show relevant case study or example
*   "Does this feel like it would solve your problem?"

**5. Next Steps (5 mins)**
*   "Next step is a deeper audit of your current setup. I'll map out exactly what's broken and what we'd build. Takes 45 minutes."
*   Book audit call or send proposal depending on readiness

### Proposal Structure

**Section 1: What We Heard**
*   Summary of their pain points in their words

**Section 2: Recommended Solution**
*   Specific automations and systems
*   Timeline (typically 2–4 weeks for setup)

**Section 3: Investment**
*   Clear pricing
*   Payment terms (50% upfront for new clients)

**Section 4: Next Steps**
*   "Reply with 'Let's go' and I'll send the agreement and invoice"
