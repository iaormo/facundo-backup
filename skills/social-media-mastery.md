# Social Media Mastery - Core Skill
_Universal frameworks for strategy, creative, media buying, and growth_

## Skill Pillars

### 1. STRATEGY ARCHITECTURE
**Platform Selection Matrix**
- B2B: LinkedIn primary, Twitter/X secondary, YouTube long-form
- B2C mass market: Meta (FB/IG) primary, TikTok for under-35s
- Visual products: Instagram, Pinterest, TikTok
- Local service: Meta + Google Local
- High-ticket: LinkedIn + YouTube + retargeting

**Content Pillars (Every Client Needs These)**
1. Authority/Education (40%) - prove expertise
2. Social Proof (25%) - results, testimonials, case studies  
3. Behind the Scenes (20%) - humanize the brand
4. Direct Offer/Promotion (15%) - clear CTAs

**Posting Rhythms**
- LinkedIn: 3-5x/week (Tue-Thu peak)
- Instagram: 4-7x/week (stories daily)
- TikTok: 1-2x/day if resource allows
- Twitter/X: 1-3x/day
- YouTube: 1x/week minimum for growth

### 2. CREATIVE PRODUCTION

**The Hook Formula (First 3 Seconds)**
Pattern interrupt + Specific promise + Curiosity gap

Examples:
- "I spent $50K on ads so you don't have to..."
- "Stop doing [common mistake]. Do this instead..."
- "The real reason your [result] isn't working..."

**Copy Frameworks**

PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution):
- Problem: "Your ads are burning budget with zero sales"
- Agitation: "Every day you wait, competitors are stealing your customers"
- Solution: "Here's the exact 3-step fix..."

AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action):
- Hook attention
- Build interest with specifics
- Create desire with transformation
- Clear action step

**Visual Standards**
- Native > Polished (iPhone content often outperforms studio)
- Faces > Products (human connection drives engagement)
- Movement > Static (video > carousel > single image)
- Text on video: Large, high contrast, 3-5 words max per frame

### 3. MEDIA BUYING & ADS

**Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)**

Campaign Structure:
- TOFU (Top of Funnel): Awareness, broad targeting, video views
- MOFU (Middle): Engagement, traffic, lead magnets
- BOFU (Bottom): Conversions, retargeting, offers

Best Practices:
- Start with Advantage+ Shopping if e-commerce
- Use broad targeting + algorithm (don't over-restrict)
- 3-5 ads per ad set for testing
- $20-50/day minimum for learning phase per ad set
- Wait 3-5 days before making major changes

**Google Ads**

Search Campaigns:
- Exact match keywords first
- Negative keywords critical
- Quality Score > everything
- Ad extensions mandatory

YouTube Ads:
- 15-30 second non-skippable for awareness
- 6 second bumpers for remarketing
- In-feed for consideration phase

**TikTok Ads**
- Spark Ads (boost organic) perform best
- Sound-on creative essential
- Trending sounds = algorithm boost
- Native style (vertical, selfie, casual)

**Universal Metrics to Track**
- CTR (Click-Through Rate): 1-2% average, 3%+ good
- CPC (Cost Per Click): Benchmark by industry
- CPM (Cost Per 1000 impressions): Measures efficiency
- CPA (Cost Per Acquisition): Target = 1/3 of customer LTV
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend): 3x+ healthy for most businesses

### 4. FUNNEL ARCHITECTURE

**Simple Lead Gen Funnel**
Ad → Landing Page → Lead Magnet → Email Sequence → Offer

**E-commerce Funnel**
Ad → Product Page → Add to Cart → Checkout → Upsell → Retarget

**High-Ticket Funnel**
Content Ads → Lead Magnet → Value Sequence → Application → Call → Close

**Retargeting Layers (Never Skip)**
1. Page visitors (7, 30, 90 days)
2. Video viewers (50%, 75%, 95%)
3. Engaged users (post engagers, IG profile visits)
4. Cart abandoners (highest intent)
5. Past customers (upsell/cross-sell)

---

### 5. TESTING FRAMEWORK

**Creative Testing (Always Be Testing)**
- Test hooks first (biggest lever)
- Test thumbnails/images second
- Test copy third
- One variable at a time

**Audience Testing**
- Interest-based vs. Lookalike vs. Broad
- Test 3-5 audiences simultaneously
- Kill losers at 3x CPA without conversions
- Scale winners by 20% every 3-5 days

**Offer Testing**
- Price points
- Bonuses/incentives
- Risk reversal (guarantees)
- Urgency mechanisms
