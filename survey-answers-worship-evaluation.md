### MUSIC MINISTRY FEEDBACK

**How would you rate the music/worship team?**
★★★★☆ (4/5) - Good

**Comments on the music ministry:**
The worship team did a great job leading the congregation. The song selection was appropriate and the musicians were skilled. 

*One area for improvement:* There was a noticeable blending issue at the end of the second song where the vocalists attempted to harmonize but it didn't quite come together. It might help to have a quick sound check or brief practice on those transition moments before the service starts. Overall though, the team ministered well and the congregation was able to worship freely.

---

### KIDS MINISTRY FEEDBACK

**How would you rate the kids ministry experience?**
★★★★☆ (4/5) - Good

**Comments on the kids ministry:**
The kids seemed to enjoy their time and the volunteers were attentive and caring. The lessons were engaging and age-appropriate.

*One area for improvement:* We had a situation where a child had an accident (pooped in his pants) and unfortunately we didn't have tissues available at that moment while everyone was looking. It would be really helpful to ensure we have tissues and basic supplies readily available in the kids area for times like these. Perhaps a small emergency kit with wipes, tissues, and a change of clothes could be kept on hand. Otherwise, the kids ministry team handled the situation well.

### USHERING / OPERATIONS FEEDBACK
